About the Author

Learn all about the author behind this sweet little summer diary! You may think my journal entries tell all, but there's a lot more to me than first meets the eye. Click through the following categories to get to know me better.

If there’s one thing I love, it’s food. I swear, half my best summer memories involve eating something amazing.

My absolute favorite food has to be pizza—classic, I know, but hear me out. There’s just something perfect about crispy crust, gooey cheese, and endless toppings. Plus, pizza nights always mean hanging out with friends, playing games, and fighting over the last slice (which, let’s be real, always belongs to me).

Next up is watermelon, because nothing screams summer more than biting into a cold, juicy slice on a hot day. It’s refreshing, messy, and somehow tastes even better when eaten outside. The only downside is when you accidentally drop some and suddenly you’re in a battle with ants. Still worth it, though.

And finally, mac and cheese—pure comfort food. Whether it’s homemade, boxed, or fancy restaurant-style, I’ll take it in any form. There’s just something magical about the combination of pasta and melted cheese. It’s warm, cozy, and perfect for lazy days when I just want to curl up with a good book or binge-watch my favorite shows.

Food honestly makes every day better, and I’m always looking for new favorites. Maybe this summer, I’ll discover my next obsession!

Music is basically the soundtrack to my summer, and I don’t think I’ve gone a single day without blasting my favorite songs. Every moment feels better with the right music—whether I’m relaxing at home, hanging out with friends, or daydreaming about my next big adventure.

My absolute favorite song right now is "APT." by Rose and Bruno Mars. It’s got the perfect mix of chill vibes and upbeat energy, making it great for summer nights. I imagine driving down an open road, windows down, warm breeze in my hair, with this song playing in the background—pure freedom!

Another song I can’t stop listening to is "Cruel Summer". It’s one of those tracks that instantly makes me feel nostalgic, even though summer isn’t over yet. I love songs that capture that bittersweet feeling of knowing time is passing but wanting to hold onto the moment forever.

Honestly, I don’t know what I’d do without music—it makes everything feel more alive. Who knows, maybe I’ll find even more favorites before summer’s over!

Games are basically my favorite way to spend time, whether I’m competing with friends, playing solo, or convincing my family to join in on the fun. There’s just something about getting totally wrapped up in a game—whether it’s strategizing, laughing, or chasing that big win.

One of my favorite board games has to be **Catan**. It’s all about trading, building, and coming up with sneaky strategies to win. I love how every game plays out differently, and how things can change with just one smart move. It’s competitive, but also a game where you have to think ahead, which makes it super fun.

When it comes to card games, **Uno** will always have a special place in my heart. There’s nothing better than watching someone’s face when you hit them with a +4 card. It’s chaotic, fast-paced, and guaranteed to cause some hilarious moments.

For sports, I’m all about **basketball**. Whether I’m shooting hoops with friends at the park or watching a big game, it’s easily one of my favorite ways to burn off energy and have fun.

And of course, video games! I’m obsessed with **Minecraft**—it’s the ultimate game for creativity. I love building, exploring, and playing survival mode with friends. No matter how many hours I put in, there’s always something new to discover.

Whether it’s rolling dice, shuffling cards, scoring goals, or adventuring in a game world, I love every second of it. Games make every day more exciting!
