Summer Diary

Hey everyone! Welcome to my summer diary website—where I’ll be documenting all the fun, weird, and exciting things that happen over the next couple of months. I got this idea after realizing that summer always flies by, and I barely remember half of what I did by the time school starts again. So, instead of letting my adventures slip away, I’m going to write them down, snap pictures, and share the ups and downs of summer break in real time! Maybe someday I’ll look back and laugh at all the silly moments (or cringe at them—who knows?).

This summer, I have a pretty solid mix of plans: some days will be full of sun, swimming, and ice cream, while others might be spent indoors binge-watching movies or finally picking up a new hobby (maybe skateboarding?). I’m hoping to go on a few trips with my family, spend more time outside, and challenge myself to try new things. Oh, and I’ve got a reading list to tackle too—because even though school is out, I still love getting lost in a good book.

I’ll be updating this site regularly with blog posts, photos, and maybe even a few fun videos if I get brave enough to record them. It’ll be messy, honest, and totally my own space to capture everything as it happens. If you want to follow along, welcome! Let’s make this summer one to remember. ☀️
